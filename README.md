## Features

What fast vpn gets right (and what you should verify on your own network):

- **High speed**: snappy browsing and quick region switches when servers aren’t overloaded
- **Stable connection**: fewer random drops during long sessions
- **AES-256 encryption**: strong baseline for protecting traffic on public Wi‑Fi
- **No-Logs policy**: privacy posture aimed at minimizing stored activity
- **Kill Switch**: cuts traffic if the tunnel drops (prevents accidental exposure)
- **Global Servers**: more location options and better odds of finding a fast nearby route
